CJC-1295 No DAC (5mg) + Ipamorelin (5mg)

Format: Lyophilized Powder Blend (10mg Total Peptide Weight)

🧬 Mechanism of Action

A synergistic dual-peptide combination designed to maximize endogenous Growth Hormone (GH) release without depleting cellular stores.

  • CJC-1295 No DAC (Mod GRF 1-29): A Growth Hormone-Releasing Hormone (GHRH) analogue. It mimics natural GHRH to signal the pituitary gland to produce GH.
  • Ipamorelin: A highly selective Growth Hormone Secretagogue (GHS) and ghrelin receptor agonist. It triggers the amplified release (pulse) of the produced GH while suppressing somatostatin (the hormone that blocks GH release).
  • Synergy: Administering a GHRH and a GHS together creates an amplified release response significantly greater than using either compound alone.

🧪 Reconstitution Guide

  • Diluent: Add 2.5 mL of Bacteriostatic (BAC) Water gently down the side of the vial.
  • Mixing: Do not shake. Swirl gently until the solution is completely clear.
  • Final Concentration: 2mg of CJC + 2mg of Ipa per 1 mL (equivalent to 100mcg of EACH compound per 5 units on a standard U-100 syringe).
  • Storage: Store in refrigerator at 36°F–46°F (2°C–8°C). Highly fragile; protect from light and mechanical agitation.

📅 Protocol & Dosing

Frequency: Subcutaneous injection administered 1 to 2 times daily. Best taken 5 days on, 2 days off to prevent pituitary receptor desensitization.

Timing Protocol: Must be administered on an empty stomach (at least 2–3 hours after a meal, and at least 20–30 minutes before consuming food or caloric beverages) to prevent insulin from blunting the GH pulse.

⚠️ Safety & Side Effects

  • Common: Transient head rush or facial flushing immediately following injection (typically resolves within 10–20 minutes), mild lethargy or increased deep sleep quality, transient water retention, and mild injection site redness.
  • Note: Unlike traditional CJC-1295 with DAC, the "No DAC" version has a short half-life (~30 minutes), creating a clean, natural spike rather than an unnatural, continuous bleed of growth hormone.

CRITICAL WARNINGS:

  • Ensure rigid fasting windows before and after administration to maintain protocol efficacy.
  • Discontinue use if experiencing persistent joint pain or severe numbness in hands (signs of excessive fluid retention/GH levels).
